Ye foundation module hai.
Agar profile intelligence strong nahi hai, to baki saare recommendations weak lagenge.

Is module ka main kaam

Student ka 360-degree profile banana:

academic profile
financial profile
aspiration profile
behavior profile
journey stage profile
Isme kya inputs aayenge
Academic inputs
current degree
branch/stream
CGPA / percentage
backlog history
year of graduation
test scores like IELTS/GRE/CAT if relevant
Aspiration inputs
target country
preferred course
domestic ya abroad
dream universities
budget tolerance
timeline: next intake / next year / exploring
Financial inputs
family income band
savings available
co-applicant availability
education budget range
scholarship expectation
Behavioral inputs
kis page pe ज्यादा time spend kar raha hai
kaunse countries compare kar raha hai
kitni baar ROI calculator use kiya
loan section me gaya ya nahi
chatbot me kya puch raha hai
Is module ka output kya hoga

Ek student profile graph / profile object:

ambition score
readiness stage
affordability band
likely destination preference
academic strength score
financing intent score
Ye kyun important hai

Iske bina personalization fake lagegi.
Ye module baaki modules ko signal deta hai:

kis student ko career advice dikhani hai
kis student ko scholarship content dikhana hai
kis student ko loan pre-check dikhana hai
Judges ko kaise explain karna

“We do not treat every student the same. Our profile intelligence layer continuously updates the user’s academic, financial, and behavioral profile to personalize the full journey.”

2) AI Career Navigator Module

Ye top-funnel engagement ka sabse visible feature hoga.
Problem statement me examples diye gaye hain like AI Career Navigator, admission predictor, timeline generator, conversational mentor. Ye wahi module hai jo platform ko sticky banata hai.

Is module ka main kaam

Student ko answer dena:

mujhe kaunsa course choose karna chahiye?
kaunsa country meri profile ke liye best hai?
kaunsi universities realistic / target / ambitious hain?
meri profile se future salary aur ROI kaisa ho sakta hai?
Module ke sub-functions
A. Country Recommendation Engine
Kya karta hai

Student ke profile ke basis par countries rank karta hai.

Ranking factors
academic fit
budget fit
visa friendliness
post-study work opportunity
field-specific demand
salary potential
immigration friendliness
loan need vs affordability
Output

Example:

UK: strong fit, fast 1-year masters, moderate ROI
Germany: low tuition, high affordability, moderate admit difficulty
Canada: good post-study route, but recent policy risk
Ireland: strong tech job market, rising relevance
Why powerful

Sirf “top countries” nahi batata, why this country fits you bhi batata.

B. Course Recommendation Engine
Kya karta hai

Student ke background aur career goals ke basis par courses suggest karta hai.

Example

Input:

B.Tech IT
CGPA 8.0
interest in analytics + AI

Output:

MS Data Science
MSc Business Analytics
MS Information Systems
Isme AI ka role

LLM user ke free-text interest ko structured career intent me convert karega.

C. University Shortlisting Engine
Kya karta hai

Universities ko 3 buckets me divide karta hai:

ambitious
target
safe
Ranking factors
academic threshold
tuition
employability
international student outcomes
scholarship probability
location-based job access
Output

A shortlist with reasoning:

why this university
expected cost
likely admit probability
placement/salary outlook
D. Career Path Explanation Layer
Kya karta hai

Student ko “course → job role → salary path” dikhata hai.

Example

MS in Data Science → Data Analyst → BI Analyst → ML Associate

Why this matters

Student ko clarity milti hai. Engagement increases.

E. Conversational Mentor / Copilot
Kya karta hai

Chatbot ki tarah doubts clear karta hai:

UK ya Germany?
MBA ya MiM?
IELTS kab dena chahiye?
low CGPA me kya options hain?
Ye sirf FAQ bot nahi होना chahiye

Isko student context pata hoga.
Agar user ki profile saved hai to bot बोले:

“Tumhare 8.1 CGPA aur one internship ke basis par Germany ka public university route affordable ho sakta hai.”
Judges ke saamne is module ki line

“Our Career Navigator is not a search tool. It is a personalized decision-support engine that helps students discover the most suitable education paths based on academic fit, affordability, and career outcomes.”

3) ROI / Outcome Intelligence Engine

Ye winning module hai.
Yahin se tumhara solution generic study-abroad platform se alag banega.

Problem statement me clearly ROI calculator aur financing journey integrate karne ki demand hai. Aur agar tum placement/salary logic add karte ho to ye aur powerful ho jata hai.

Is module ka main kaam

Student aur lender dono ko answer dena:

is course ka financial sense banta hai ya nahi?
expected salary kya ho sakti hai?
job milne me kitna time lag sakta hai?
repayment realistic hai ya risky?
Is module ke sub-functions
A. Total Cost Estimator
Kya karta hai

Sirf tuition nahi, full study cost estimate karta hai:

tuition fee
living cost
visa/application fee
exam fee
travel
buffer
Output

Country/program wise total cost range:

Tuition: 18L
Living: 9L
Misc: 3L
Total: 30L
B. Salary Prediction Engine
Kya karta hai

Course + country + university type + market demand ke basis par expected salary band predict karta hai.

Output
conservative salary
median expected salary
strong outcome salary

Example:

₹8–12 LPA equivalent start band
Why useful

Student ke liye aspiration
Lender ke liye repayment signal

C. Placement Timeline Predictor

Ye Problem 1 se inspired powerful addition hai.

Kya karta hai

Predict karta hai:

3 months me placement chance
6 months me placement chance
12 months me placement chance
Inputs
academics
internship quality
field demand
institute strength
job market trend
certifications/projects
Output
placement within 3 months: 62%
within 6 months: 84%
within 12 months: 93%
D. Placement Risk Classification
Kya karta hai

User ko classify karta hai:

low risk
medium risk
high risk of delayed placement
Risk explanation
low internship exposure
lower demand specialization
weaker institute outcomes
strong academics but weak practical exposure
Why this wins

Kyuki tum blind financing nahi kar rahe, outcome-aware financing dikha rahe ho.

E. ROI Calculator
Kya karta hai

Compare karta hai:

total education cost
expected salary
expected loan amount
repayment window
break-even period
Output
ROI strong / moderate / weak
payback estimate: 3.8 years
recommended loan band: 18–24L
F. Explainability Layer
Kya karta hai

Prediction ko human-readable banata hai.

Example:
“Your profile shows moderate employability risk because you have strong academics but limited internship exposure and are targeting a field with currently moderate hiring demand.”

Judges ke liye ye bahut important hai

Black-box ML nahi dikhna chahiye.
Explainable hona chahiye.
